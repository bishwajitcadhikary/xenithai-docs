# Languages

Here you can manage the languages that are available in your application. You can also add new languages, edit existing ones, and delete them.

### Step 1: Go to Languages Page
1. Log into your Admin Panel and navigate to the `Settings` section.
2. Click on the `Languages` tab.
<Image src={'/images/admin/settings/languages/1.png'} className={'bg-img'} width={1000} height={500} alt={'Image'} />

### Step 2: Create Language
1. Click on the `Create Language` button.
<Image src={'/images/admin/settings/languages/2.png'} className={'bg-img'} width={1000} height={500} alt={'Image'} />
2. Enter the coupon details.
3. Click on the `Save` button.
<Image src={'/images/admin/settings/languages/3.png'} className={'bg-img'} width={1000} height={500} alt={'Image'} />

### Step 3: Edit Language
1. Click on the `Edit` button to edit the languages.
<Image src={'/images/admin/settings/languages/4.png'} className={'bg-img'} width={1000} height={500} alt={'Image'} />
2. Edit the language details.
3. Click on the `Update Changes` button.
<Image src={'/images/admin/settings/languages/5.png'} className={'bg-img'} width={1000} height={500} alt={'Image'} />

### Step 4: Delete Language
1. Click on the `Delete` button to delete the languages.
<Image src={'/images/admin/settings/languages/6.png'} className={'bg-img'} width={1000} height={500} alt={'Image'} />

### Step 5: Bulk Delete Languages
1. Select the languages you want to delete or click on the `Select All` checkbox to select all the languages.
<Image src={'/images/admin/settings/languages/7.png'} className={'bg-img'} width={1000} height={500} alt={'Image'} />

### Step 5: Translation
1. Click on the `Translation` button to translate the languages.
<Image src={'/images/admin/settings/languages/8.png'} className={'bg-img'} width={1000} height={500} alt={'Image'} />
2. Update the phrases.
3. Click on the `Update` button.

For example, I'm going to translate the `Dashboard` phrase to `ড্যাশবোর্ড` in Bengali.
<Image src={'/images/admin/settings/languages/9.png'} className={'bg-img'} width={1000} height={500} alt={'Image'} />

### Step 6: Check Translation
1. I've switched the language to Bengali, and the `Dashboard` phrase is translated to `ড্যাশবোর্ড`.
<Image src={'/images/admin/settings/languages/10.png'} className={'bg-img'} width={1000} height={500} alt={'Image'} />
2. View the changes in the frontend.
<Image src={'/images/admin/settings/languages/11.png'} className={'bg-img'} width={1000} height={500} alt={'Image'} />
