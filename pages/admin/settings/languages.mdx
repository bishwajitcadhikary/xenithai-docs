# Languages

Here you can manage the languages that are available in your application. You can also add new languages, edit existing ones, and delete them.

### Step 1: Go to Languages Page
1. Log into your Admin Panel and navigate to the "Settings" section.
2. Click on the "Languages" tab.
[![image](/images/admin/settings/languages/1.png)](/images/admin/settings/languages/1.png)

### Step 2: Create Language
1. Click on the "Create Language" button.
[![image](/images/admin/settings/languages/2.png)](/images/admin/settings/languages/2.png)
2. Enter the coupon details.
3. Click on the "Save" button.
[![image](/images/admin/settings/languages/3.png)](/images/admin/settings/languages/3.png)

### Step 3: Edit Language
1. Click on the "Edit" button to edit the languages.
[![image](/images/admin/settings/languages/4.png)](/images/admin/settings/languages/4.png)
2. Edit the language details.
3. Click on the "Update Changes" button.
[![image](/images/admin/settings/languages/5.png)](/images/admin/settings/languages/5.png)


### Step 4: Delete Language
1. Click on the "Delete" button to delete the languages.
[![image](/images/admin/settings/languages/6.png)](/images/admin/settings/languages/6.png)

### Step 5: Bulk Delete Languages
1. Select the languages you want to delete or click on the "Select All" checkbox to select all the languages.
[![image](/images/admin/settings/languages/7.png)](/images/admin/settings/languages/7.png)

### Step 5: Translation
1. Click on the "Translation" button to translate the languages.
[![image](/images/admin/settings/languages/8.png)](/images/admin/settings/languages/8.png)
2. Update the phrases.
3. Click on the "Update" button.

For example, I'm going to translate the "Dashboard" phrase to "ড্যাশবোর্ড" in Bengali.
[![image](/images/admin/settings/languages/9.png)](/images/admin/settings/languages/9.png)

I've switched the language to Bengali, and the "Dashboard" phrase is translated to "ড্যাশবোর্ড".
