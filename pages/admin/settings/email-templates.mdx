# Email Templates

Here you can manage the email templates that are available in your application.

### Step 1: Go to Email Templates Page
1. Log into your Admin Panel and navigate to the "Settings" section.
2. Click on the "Email Templates" tab.
[![image](/images/admin/settings/email-templates/1.png)](/images/admin/settings/email-templates/1.png)

### Step 3: Edit Email Template
1. Click on the "Edit" button to edit the email template.
[![image](/images/admin/settings/email-templates/2.png)](/images/admin/settings/email-templates/2.png)
2. Edit the email template details.
3. Use the "shortcodes" to add dynamic content to your email templates.
4. Click on the "Update Changes" button.
[![image](/images/admin/settings/email-templates/3.png)](/images/admin/settings/email-templates/3.png)
