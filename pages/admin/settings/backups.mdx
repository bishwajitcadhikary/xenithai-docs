# Backups

Here you can manage the backups that are available in your application.

### Step 1: Go to Backups Page
1. Log into your Admin Panel and navigate to the "Settings" section.
2. Click on the "Backups" tab.
[![image](/images/admin/settings/backups/1.png)](/images/admin/settings/backups/1.png)

### Step 2: Create a Backup
1. Click on the "Create Backup" button.
[![image](/images/admin/settings/backups/2.png)](/images/admin/settings/backups/2.png)
2. Select the backup type.
3. Enter password if you want to encrypt the backup.
4. Enter email address if you want to notify someone when the backup is ready.
5. Click on the "Create" button.
[![image](/images/admin/settings/backups/3.png)](/images/admin/settings/backups/3.png)

### Step 3: Download a Backup
1. Click on the "Download" button.
[![image](/images/admin/settings/backups/4.png)](/images/admin/settings/backups/4.png)
