### Step 1: Go to SMTP Page
1. Log into your Admin Panel and navigate to the "Settings" section.
2. Click on the "General" tab.
[![image](/images/admin/settings/smtp/1.png)](/images/admin/settings/smtp/1.png)

### Step 2: Fill in the SMTP Information
1. Fill in the SMTP information for your email provider.
2. Click "Update Changes" to save your settings.
[![image](/images/admin/settings/smtp/2.png)](/images/admin/settings/smtp/2.png)

### Step 3: Test the SMTP Settings
1. Fill in the email address you would like to send a test email to.
2. Click "Send Test Email" to send a test email.
[![image](/images/admin/settings/smtp/3.png)](/images/admin/settings/smtp/3.png)

### Step 4: Check your Email
1. Check your email to see if you received the test email.
