# General Settings

### Step 1: Go to General Settings Page
1. Log into your Admin Panel and navigate to the `Settings` section.
2. Click on the `General` tab.
<Image src={'/images/admin/settings/general/1.png'} className={'bg-img'} width={1000} height={500} alt={'Image'} />

### Step 2: Fill in the General Settings
#### Company Information
This information will be used in the Invoice and other documents.
<Image src={'/images/admin/settings/general/2.png'} className={'bg-img'} width={1000} height={500} alt={'Image'} />

#### Site Information
This information will be used in the header and footer of the site.
<Image src={'/images/admin/settings/general/3.png'} className={'bg-img'} width={1000} height={500} alt={'Image'} />

#### Preferences
This information will be used to set the default values for the site.
<Image src={'/images/admin/settings/general/4.png'} className={'bg-img'} width={1000} height={500} alt={'Image'} />

#### Google Recaptcha
1. Go to [Google Recaptcha](https://www.google.com/recaptcha/admin) and register your site.
2. Copy the Site Key and Secret Key and paste them in the respective fields.
Note: We recommend using the `reCAPTCHA v2` option.
<Image src={'/images/admin/settings/general/5.png'} className={'bg-img'} width={1000} height={500} alt={'Image'} />

#### Cookie Consent
1. We have integrated the Cookie Consent plugin to help you comply with the EU Cookie Law.
2. You can enable/disable the plugin by clicking on the toggle button.
<Image src={'/images/admin/settings/general/6.png'} className={'bg-img'} width={1000} height={500} alt={'Image'} />

#### IP Info API
1. We have integrated the IP Info API to help you get the location of the user.
2. Go to [IP Info API](https://ipinfo.io/) and register your site.
3. Copy the Access Token and paste it in the respective field.
<Image src={'/images/admin/settings/general/7.png'} className={'bg-img'} width={1000} height={500} alt={'Image'} />

#### Disquss Comments
1. We have integrated the Disquss Comments plugin to help you get comments on your site.
2. Go to [Disquss Comments](https://disqus.com/profile/signup/intent/) and register your site.
3. Copy the Shortname and paste it in the respective field.
<Image src={'/images/admin/settings/general/8.png'} className={'bg-img'} width={1000} height={500} alt={'Image'} />

### Step 3: Save the General Settings
1. Click on the `Update Changes` button to save the changes.
<Image src={'/images/admin/settings/general/9.png'} className={'bg-img'} width={1000} height={500} alt={'Image'} />
