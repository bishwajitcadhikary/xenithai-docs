# Roles

Here you can manage the roles that are available in your application. You can also add new roles, edit existing ones, and delete them.

### Step 1: Go to Roles Page
1. Log into your Admin Panel and navigate to the "Settings" section.
2. Click on the `Roles` tab.
<Image src={'/images/admin/settings/roles/1.png'} className={'bg-img'} width={1000} height={500} alt={'Image'} />

### Step 2: Create Role
1. Click on the `Add New Role` button.
<Image src={'/images/admin/settings/roles/2.png'} className={'bg-img'} width={1000} height={500} alt={'Image'} />
2. Enter the role details.
3. Click on the `Save` button.
<Image src={'/images/admin/settings/roles/3.png'} className={'bg-img'} width={1000} height={500} alt={'Image'} />

### Step 3: Edit Role
1. Click on the `Edit` button to edit the roles.
<Image src={'/images/admin/settings/roles/4.png'} className={'bg-img'} width={1000} height={500} alt={'Image'} />
2. Edit the role details.
3. Click on the `Update Changes` button.
<Image src={'/images/admin/settings/roles/5.png'} className={'bg-img'} width={1000} height={500} alt={'Image'} />

### Step 4: Delete Role
1. Click on the `Delete` button to delete the roles.
<Image src={'/images/admin/settings/roles/6.png'} className={'bg-img'} width={1000} height={500} alt={'Image'} />
