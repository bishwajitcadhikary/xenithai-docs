# Payment Gateways

### Step 1: Go to Payment Gateways Page
1. Log into your Admin Panel and navigate to the `Payment Gateways` section.
<Image src={'/images/admin/business/payments/1.png'} className={'bg-img'} width={1000} height={500} alt={'Image'} />
2. To set your payment gateway credentials, click on the `Edit` button next to the payment gateway you want to configure. <br/>
    For this example, we will be using the `PayPal` payment gateway.
<Image src={'/images/admin/business/payments/2.png'} className={'bg-img'} width={1000} height={500} alt={'Image'} />

### Step 2: Set your Payment Gateway Credentials
1. Enter your payment gateway credentials.
2. Change sandbox mode to `No` if you want to accept real payments.
<Image src={'/images/admin/business/payments/3.png'} className={'bg-img'} width={1000} height={500} alt={'Image'} />
