# Coupons

Here you can manage the coupons that are available in your application. You can also add new coupons, edit existing ones and delete them.

### Step 1: Go to Coupons Page
1. Log into your Admin Panel and navigate to the `Business Setup` section.
2. Click on the `Coupons` tab.
<Image src={'/images/admin/business/coupons/1.png'} className={'bg-img'} width={1000} height={500} alt={'Image'} />

### Step 2: Create Coupon
1. Click on the `Create Coupon` button.
<Image src={'/images/admin/business/coupons/2.png'} className={'bg-img'} width={1000} height={500} alt={'Image'} />
2. Enter the coupon details.
3. Click on the `Save` button.
<Image src={'/images/admin/business/coupons/3.png'} className={'bg-img'} width={1000} height={500} alt={'Image'} />

### Step 3: Edit Coupon
1. Click on the `Edit` button to edit the plan.
<Image src={'/images/admin/business/coupons/4.png'} className={'bg-img'} width={1000} height={500} alt={'Image'} />
2. Edit the plan details.
3. Click on the `Update Changes` button.
<Image src={'/images/admin/business/coupons/5.png'} className={'bg-img'} width={1000} height={500} alt={'Image'} />


### Step 4: Delete Coupon
1. Click on the `Delete` button to delete the plan.
<Image src={'/images/admin/business/coupons/6.png'} className={'bg-img'} width={1000} height={500} alt={'Image'} />

### Step 5: Bulk Delete Coupons
1. Select the coupons you want to delete or click on the `Select All` checkbox to select all the coupons.
<Image src={'/images/admin/business/coupons/7.png'} className={'bg-img'} width={1000} height={500} alt={'Image'} />
