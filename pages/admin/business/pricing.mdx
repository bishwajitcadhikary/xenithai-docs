# Subscription Plans

Here you can see the subscription plans that you have created.

### Step 1: Go to Payment Gateways Page
1. Log into your Admin Panel and navigate to the `Business Setup` section.
2. Click on the `Plan & Pricing` tab.
<Image src={'/images/admin/business/pricing/1.png'} className={'bg-img'} width={1000} height={500} alt={'Image'} />

### Step 2: Create Subscription Plan
1. Click on the `Create Plan` button.
<Image src={'/images/admin/business/pricing/2.png'} className={'bg-img'} width={1000} height={500} alt={'Image'} />
2. Enter the plan name, price, description and other details.
3. Click on the `Save` button.
<Image src={'/images/admin/business/pricing/3.png'} className={'bg-img'} width={1000} height={500} alt={'Image'} />

### Step 3: Edit Subscription Plan
1. Click on the `Edit` button to edit the plan.
<Image src={'/images/admin/business/pricing/4.png'} className={'bg-img'} width={1000} height={500} alt={'Image'} />
2. Edit the plan details.
3. Click on the `Update Changes` button.
<Image src={'/images/admin/business/pricing/5.png'} className={'bg-img'} width={1000} height={500} alt={'Image'} />
