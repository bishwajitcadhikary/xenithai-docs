# Blogs & News

Here you can manage the blogs that are available in your application. You can also add new blogs, edit existing ones, and delete them.

### Step 1: Go to Blogs Page
1. Log into your Admin Panel and navigate to the `Customization` section.
2. Click on the `Blogs` tab.
<Image src={'/images/admin/customization/blogs/1.png'} className={'bg-img'} width={1000} height={500} alt={'Image'} />

### Step 2: Create Blog
1. Click on the `Create Blog` button.
<Image src={'/images/admin/customization/blogs/2.png'} className={'bg-img'} width={1000} height={500} alt={'Image'} />
2. Enter the coupon details.
3. Click on the `Save` button.
<Image src={'/images/admin/customization/blogs/3.png'} className={'bg-img'} width={1000} height={500} alt={'Image'} />

### Step 3: Edit Blog
1. Click on the `Edit` button to edit the blog.
<Image src={'/images/admin/customization/blogs/4.png'} className={'bg-img'} width={1000} height={500} alt={'Image'} />
2. Edit the blog details.
3. Click on the `Update Changes` button.
<Image src={'/images/admin/customization/blogs/5.png'} className={'bg-img'} width={1000} height={500} alt={'Image'} />

### Step 4: Delete Blog
1. Click on the `Delete` button to delete the blog.
<Image src={'/images/admin/customization/blogs/6.png'} className={'bg-img'} width={1000} height={500} alt={'Image'} />

### Step 5: Bulk Delete Blogs
1. Select the blogs you want to delete or click on the `Select All` checkbox to select all the blogs.
<Image src={'/images/admin/customization/blogs/7.png'} className={'bg-img'} width={1000} height={500} alt={'Image'} />
