# Blogs & News

Here you can manage the blogs that are available in your application. You can also add new blogs, edit existing ones, and delete them.

### Step 1: Go to Blogs Page
1. Log into your Admin Panel and navigate to the "Customization" section.
2. Click on the "Blogs" tab.
[![image](/images/admin/customization/blogs/1.png)](/images/admin/customization/blogs/1.png)

### Step 2: Create Blog
1. Click on the "Create Blog" button.
[![image](/images/admin/customization/blogs/2.png)](/images/admin/customization/blogs/2.png)
2. Enter the coupon details.
3. Click on the "Save" button.
[![image](/images/admin/customization/blogs/3.png)](/images/admin/customization/blogs/3.png)

### Step 3: Edit Blog
1. Click on the "Edit" button to edit the blog.
[![image](/images/admin/customization/blogs/4.png)](/images/admin/customization/blogs/4.png)
2. Edit the blog details.
3. Click on the "Update Changes" button.
[![image](/images/admin/customization/blogs/5.png)](/images/admin/customization/blogs/5.png)


### Step 4: Delete Blog
1. Click on the "Delete" button to delete the blog.
[![image](/images/admin/customization/blogs/6.png)](/images/admin/customization/blogs/6.png)

### Step 5: Bulk Delete Blogs
1. Select the blogs you want to delete or click on the "Select All" checkbox to select all the blogs.
[![image](/images/admin/customization/blogs/7.png)](/images/admin/customization/blogs/7.png)
