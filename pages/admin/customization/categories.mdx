# Blog Categories

Here you can manage the categories that are available in your application. You can also add new categories, edit existing ones, and delete them.

### Step 1: Go to Blog Categories
1. Log into your Admin Panel and navigate to the `Customization` section.
2. Click on the `Blog Categories` tab.
<Image src={'/images/admin/customization/categories/1.png'} className={'bg-img'} width={1000} height={500} alt={'Image'} />

### Step 2: Create Category
1. Click on the `Create Category` button.
<Image src={'/images/admin/customization/categories/2.png'} className={'bg-img'} width={1000} height={500} alt={'Image'} />
2. Enter the coupon details.
3. Click on the `Save` button.
<Image src={'/images/admin/customization/categories/3.png'} className={'bg-img'} width={1000} height={500} alt={'Image'} />

### Step 3: Edit Category
1. Click on the `Edit` button to edit the category.
<Image src={'/images/admin/customization/categories/4.png'} className={'bg-img'} width={1000} height={500} alt={'Image'} />
2. Edit the category details.
3. Click on the `Update Changes` button.
<Image src={'/images/admin/customization/categories/5.png'} className={'bg-img'} width={1000} height={500} alt={'Image'} />

### Step 4: Delete Category
1. Click on the `Delete` button to delete the category.
<Image src={'/images/admin/customization/categories/6.png'} className={'bg-img'} width={1000} height={500} alt={'Image'} />

### Step 5: Bulk Delete Categories
1. Select the categories you want to delete or click on the `Select All` checkbox to select all the categories.
<Image src={'/images/admin/customization/categories/7.png'} className={'bg-img'} width={1000} height={500} alt={'Image'} />
