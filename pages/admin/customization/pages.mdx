# Pages

Here you can manage the pages that are available in your application. You can also add new pages, edit existing ones, and delete them.

### Step 1: Go to Pages Page
1. Log into your Admin Panel and navigate to the "Customization" section.
2. Click on the `Pages` tab.
<Image src={'/images/admin/customization/pages/1.png'} className={'bg-img'} width={1000} height={500} alt={'Image'} />

### Step 2: Create Page
1. Click on the `Create Page` button.
<Image src={'/images/admin/customization/pages/2.png'} className={'bg-img'} width={1000} height={500} alt={'Image'} />
2. Enter the coupon details.
3. Click on the `Save` button.
<Image src={'/images/admin/customization/pages/3.png'} className={'bg-img'} width={1000} height={500} alt={'Image'} />

### Step 3: Edit Page
1. Click on the `Edit` button to edit the page.
<Image src={'/images/admin/customization/pages/4.png'} className={'bg-img'} width={1000} height={500} alt={'Image'} />
2. Edit the page details.
3. Click on the `Update Changes` button.
<Image src={'/images/admin/customization/pages/5.png'} className={'bg-img'} width={1000} height={500} alt={'Image'} />


### Step 4: Delete Page
1. Click on the `Delete` button to delete the page.
<Image src={'/images/admin/customization/pages/6.png'} className={'bg-img'} width={1000} height={500} alt={'Image'} />

### Step 5: Bulk Delete Pages
1. Select the pages you want to delete or click on the `Select All` checkbox to select all the pages.
<Image src={'/images/admin/customization/pages/7.png'} className={'bg-img'} width={1000} height={500} alt={'Image'} />
