# Pages

Here you can manage the pages that are available in your application. You can also add new pages, edit existing ones, and delete them.

### Step 1: Go to Pages Page
1. Log into your Admin Panel and navigate to the "Customization" section.
2. Click on the "Blogs" tab.
[![image](/images/admin/customization/pages/1.png)](/images/admin/customization/pages/1.png)

### Step 2: Create Page
1. Click on the "Create Page" button.
[![image](/images/admin/customization/pages/2.png)](/images/admin/customization/pages/2.png)
2. Enter the coupon details.
3. Click on the "Save" button.
[![image](/images/admin/customization/pages/3.png)](/images/admin/customization/pages/3.png)

### Step 3: Edit Page
1. Click on the "Edit" button to edit the page.
[![image](/images/admin/customization/pages/4.png)](/images/admin/customization/pages/4.png)
2. Edit the page details.
3. Click on the "Update Changes" button.
[![image](/images/admin/customization/pages/5.png)](/images/admin/customization/pages/5.png)


### Step 4: Delete Page
1. Click on the "Delete" button to delete the page.
[![image](/images/admin/customization/pages/6.png)](/images/admin/customization/pages/6.png)

### Step 5: Bulk Delete Pages
1. Select the pages you want to delete or click on the "Select All" checkbox to select all the pages.
[![image](/images/admin/customization/pages/7.png)](/images/admin/customization/pages/7.png)
