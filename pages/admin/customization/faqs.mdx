# Frequently Asked Questions

Here you can manage the faqs that are available in your application. You can also add new faqs, edit existing ones, and delete them. This section is shown to the users in the `Help` section of the application.
<Image src={'/images/admin/customization/faqs/preview.png'} className={'bg-img'} width={1000} height={500} alt={'Image'} />

### Step 1: Go to FAQs Page
1. Log into your Admin Panel and navigate to the `Customization` section.
2. Click on the `FAQs` tab.
<Image src={'/images/admin/customization/faqs/1.png'} className={'bg-img'} width={1000} height={500} alt={'Image'} />

### Step 2: Create FAQ
1. Click on the `Create FAQ` button.
<Image src={'/images/admin/customization/faqs/2.png'} className={'bg-img'} width={1000} height={500} alt={'Image'} />
2. Enter the coupon details.
3. Click on the `Save` button.
<Image src={'/images/admin/customization/faqs/3.png'} className={'bg-img'} width={1000} height={500} alt={'Image'} />

### Step 3: Edit FAQ
1. Click on the `Edit` button to edit the faqs.
<Image src={'/images/admin/customization/faqs/4.png'} className={'bg-img'} width={1000} height={500} alt={'Image'} />
2. Edit the faqs details.
3. Click on the `Update Changes` button.
<Image src={'/images/admin/customization/faqs/5.png'} className={'bg-img'} width={1000} height={500} alt={'Image'} />

### Step 4: Delete FAQ
1. Click on the `Delete` button to delete the faqs.
<Image src={'/images/admin/customization/faqs/6.png'} className={'bg-img'} width={1000} height={500} alt={'Image'} />

### Step 5: Bulk Delete FAQs
1. Select the faqs you want to delete or click on the `Select All` checkbox to select all the faqs.
<Image src={'/images/admin/customization/faqs/7.png'} className={'bg-img'} width={1000} height={500} alt={'Image'} />
