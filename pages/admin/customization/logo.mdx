# Logo

### Step 1: Go to Logo Page
1. Log into your Admin Panel and navigate to the `Customization` section.
2. Click on the `Logo` tab.
<Image src={'/images/admin/customization/logo/1.png'} className={'bg-img'} width={1000} height={500} alt={'Image'} />

### Step 2: Update Logos
1. Click on your desired section from the list. In this example, we will be updating the `FAQs` section.
2. Update Light Logo, Dark Logo, Favicon, and Breadcrumb Background Image.
3. Click `Update Changes` to save your changes.
<Image src={'/images/admin/customization/logo/2.png'} className={'bg-img'} width={1000} height={500} alt={'Image'} />

### Step 3: View Changes
1. Go to your website and refresh the page to view your changes.
