# Frequently Asked Questions

Here you can manage the AI Features that are available in your application. You can also add new AI Features, edit existing ones, and delete them.
[![image](/images/admin/customization/ai-features/preview.png)](/images/admin/customization/ai-features/preview.png)

### Step 1: Go to AI Features Page
1. Log into your Admin Panel and navigate to the "Customization" section.
2. Click on the "FAQs" tab.
[![image](/images/admin/customization/ai-features/1.png)](/images/admin/customization/ai-features/1.png)

### Step 2: Create AI Feature
1. Click on the "Create AI Feature" button.
[![image](/images/admin/customization/ai-features/2.png)](/images/admin/customization/ai-features/2.png)
2. Enter the coupon details.
3. Click on the "Save" button.
[![image](/images/admin/customization/ai-features/3.png)](/images/admin/customization/ai-features/3.png)

### Step 3: Edit AI Feature
1. Click on the "Edit" button to edit the ai features.
[![image](/images/admin/customization/ai-features/4.png)](/images/admin/customization/ai-features/4.png)
2. Edit the ai features details.
3. Click on the "Update Changes" button.
[![image](/images/admin/customization/ai-features/5.png)](/images/admin/customization/ai-features/5.png)


### Step 4: Delete AI Feature
1. Click on the "Delete" button to delete the ai features.
[![image](/images/admin/customization/ai-features/6.png)](/images/admin/customization/ai-features/6.png)

### Step 5: Bulk Delete AI Features
1. Select the ai features you want to delete or click on the "Select All" checkbox to select all the ai features.
[![image](/images/admin/customization/ai-features/7.png)](/images/admin/customization/ai-features/7.png)
