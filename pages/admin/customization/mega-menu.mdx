# Mega Menu

### Step 1: Go to Mega Menu Page
1. Log into your Admin Panel and navigate to the "Customization" section.
2. Click on the "Mega Menu" tab.
[![image](/images/admin/customization/mega-menu/1.png)](/images/admin/customization/mega-menu/1.png)

### Step 2: Update Mega Menu
1. Click on the "Update Changes" button to save your changes.
[![image](/images/admin/customization/mega-menu/2.png)](/images/admin/customization/mega-menu/2.png)
