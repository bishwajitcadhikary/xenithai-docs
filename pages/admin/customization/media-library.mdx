# Media Library

Here you can manage the images that are available in your application. You can also add new image, and delete them.

### Step 1: Go to Media Library Page
1. Log into your Admin Panel and navigate to the `Customization` section.
2. Click on the `Media Library` tab.
<Image src={'/images/admin/customization/media-library/1.png'} className={'bg-img'} width={1000} height={500} alt={'Image'} />

### Step 2: Upload Images
1. Drag and drop the image or click on the `Browse` button to upload the image.
<Image src={'/images/admin/customization/media-library/2.png'} className={'bg-img'} width={1000} height={500} alt={'Image'} />

### Step 3: Delete Testimonial
1. Click on the `Delete` button to delete the media library.
<Image src={'/images/admin/customization/media-library/3.png'} className={'bg-img'} width={1000} height={500} alt={'Image'} />
