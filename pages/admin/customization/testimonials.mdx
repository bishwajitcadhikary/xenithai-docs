# Testimonials

Here you can manage the testimonials that are available in your application. You can also add new testimonials, edit existing ones, and delete them. This section is shown to the users in the `Help` section of the application.
<Image src={'/images/admin/customization/testimonials/preview.png'} className={'bg-img'} width={1000} height={500} alt={'Image'} />

### Step 1: Go to Testimonials Page
1. Log into your Admin Panel and navigate to the `Customization` section.
2. Click on the `Testimonials` tab.
<Image src={'/images/admin/customization/testimonials/1.png'} className={'bg-img'} width={1000} height={500} alt={'Image'} />

### Step 2: Create Testimonial
1. Click on the `Create Testimonial` button.
<Image src={'/images/admin/customization/testimonials/2.png'} className={'bg-img'} width={1000} height={500} alt={'Image'} />
2. Enter the coupon details.
3. Click on the `Save` button.
<Image src={'/images/admin/customization/testimonials/3.png'} className={'bg-img'} width={1000} height={500} alt={'Image'} />

### Step 3: Edit Testimonial
1. Click on the `Edit` button to edit the testimonials.
<Image src={'/images/admin/customization/testimonials/4.png'} className={'bg-img'} width={1000} height={500} alt={'Image'} />
2. Edit the testimonials details.
3. Click on the `Update Changes` button.
<Image src={'/images/admin/customization/testimonials/5.png'} className={'bg-img'} width={1000} height={500} alt={'Image'} />

### Step 4: Delete Testimonial
1. Click on the `Delete` button to delete the testimonials.
<Image src={'/images/admin/customization/testimonials/6.png'} className={'bg-img'} width={1000} height={500} alt={'Image'} />

### Step 5: Bulk Delete Testimonials
1. Select the testimonials you want to delete or click on the `Select All` checkbox to select all the testimonials.
<Image src={'/images/admin/customization/testimonials/7.png'} className={'bg-img'} width={1000} height={500} alt={'Image'} />
