# Menu Builder

### Step 1: Go to Menu Builder Page
1. Log into your Admin Panel and navigate to the "Customization" section.
2. Click on the "Menu Builder" tab.
[![image](/images/admin/customization/menu-builder/1.png)](/images/admin/customization/menu-builder/1.png)

### Step 2: Edit Menu
1. Click on the "Edit" button of the menu you want to edit.
[![image](/images/admin/customization/menu-builder/2.png)](/images/admin/customization/menu-builder/2.png)
2. Click on the "Update Changes" button to save your changes.
[![image](/images/admin/customization/menu-builder/3.png)](/images/admin/customization/menu-builder/3.png)

### Step 3: Customize Menu
1. Click on the "Customize" button of the menu you want to customize.
[![image](/images/admin/customization/menu-builder/4.png)](/images/admin/customization/menu-builder/4.png)
2. Click on the "Add to menu" button to add a new item to the menu.
[![image](/images/admin/customization/menu-builder/5.png)](/images/admin/customization/menu-builder/5.png)
