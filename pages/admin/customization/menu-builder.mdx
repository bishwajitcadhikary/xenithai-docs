# Menu Builder

### Step 1: Go to Menu Builder Page
1. Log into your Admin Panel and navigate to the `Customization` section.
2. Click on the `Menu Builder` tab.


### Header Menu - Position
1. Top Menu - `header`,
<Image src={'/images/admin/customization/menu-builder/preview-1.png'} className={'bg-img'} width={1000} height={500} alt={'Image'} />

### Footer Menus - Position
1. Quick Links - `footer_menu_one`,
2. Company - `footer_menu_two`,
3. Contact Us - `footer_menu_three`
<Image src={'/images/admin/customization/menu-builder/preview-2.png'} className={'bg-img'} width={1000} height={500} alt={'Image'} />

### Step 2: Edit Menu
1. Click on the `Edit` button of the menu you want to edit.
<Image src={'/images/admin/customization/menu-builder/2.png'} className={'bg-img'} width={1000} height={500} alt={'Image'} />
2. Click on the `Update Changes` button to save your changes.
<Image src={'/images/admin/customization/menu-builder/3.png'} className={'bg-img'} width={1000} height={500} alt={'Image'} />

### Step 3: Customize Menu
1. Click on the `Customize` button of the menu you want to customize.
<Image src={'/images/admin/customization/menu-builder/4.png'} className={'bg-img'} width={1000} height={500} alt={'Image'} />
2. Click on the `Add to menu` button to add a new item to the menu.
