# Template Categories

### Step 1: Go to Template Category Page
1. Log into your Admin Panel and navigate to the `AI Setup` section.
2. Click on the `Template Category` tab.
<Image src={'/images/admin/ai/categories/1.png'} className={'bg-img'} width={1000} height={500} alt={'Image'} />

### Step 2: Create Template Category
1. Click on the `Create Template Category` button.
<Image src={'/images/admin/ai/categories/2.png'} className={'bg-img'} width={1000} height={500} alt={'Image'} />
2. Enter the template category details.
3. Click on the `Save` button.
<Image src={'/images/admin/ai/categories/3.png'} className={'bg-img'} width={1000} height={500} alt={'Image'} />

### Step 3: Edit Template Category
1. Click on the `Edit` button to edit the template category.
<Image src={'/images/admin/ai/categories/4.png'} className={'bg-img'} width={1000} height={500} alt={'Image'} />
2. Edit the template category details.
3. Click on the `Update Changes` button.
<Image src={'/images/admin/ai/categories/5.png'} className={'bg-img'} width={1000} height={500} alt={'Image'} />

### Step 4: Delete Template Category
1. Click on the `Delete` button to delete the template category.
<Image src={'/images/admin/ai/categories/6.png'} className={'bg-img'} width={1000} height={500} alt={'Image'} />

### Step 5: Bulk Delete Template Categories
1. Select the template categories you want to delete or click on the `Select All` checkbox to select all the template categories.
<Image src={'/images/admin/ai/categories/7.png'} className={'bg-img'} width={1000} height={500} alt={'Image'} />
