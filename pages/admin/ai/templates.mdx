# Templates

### Step 1: Go to Template Page
1. Log into your Admin Panel and navigate to the `AI Setup` section.
2. Click on the `Template` tab.
<Image src={'/images/admin/ai/templates/1.png'} className={'bg-img'} width={1000} height={500} alt={'Image'} />

### Step 2: Create Template
1. Click on the `Create Template` button.
<Image src={'/images/admin/ai/templates/2.png'} className={'bg-img'} width={1000} height={500} alt={'Image'} />
2. Enter the template details.
3. Click on the `Save` button.
<Image src={'/images/admin/ai/templates/3.png'} className={'bg-img'} width={1000} height={500} alt={'Image'} />

### Step 3: Edit Template
1. Click on the `Edit` button to edit the template.
<Image src={'/images/admin/ai/templates/4.png'} className={'bg-img'} width={1000} height={500} alt={'Image'} />
2. Edit the template details.
3. Click on the `Update Changes` button.
<Image src={'/images/admin/ai/templates/5.png'} className={'bg-img'} width={1000} height={500} alt={'Image'} />

### Step 4: Delete Template
1. Click on the `Delete` button to delete the template.
<Image src={'/images/admin/ai/templates/6.png'} className={'bg-img'} width={1000} height={500} alt={'Image'} />

### Step 5: Bulk Delete Templates
1. Select the templates you want to delete or click on the `Select All` checkbox to select all the templates.
<Image src={'/images/admin/ai/templates/7.png'} className={'bg-img'} width={1000} height={500} alt={'Image'} />
