# Chat Bots

### Step 1: Go to Chat Bots Page
1. Log into your Admin Panel and navigate to the `AI Setup` section.
2. Click on the `Chat Bots` tab.
<Image src={'/images/admin/ai/bots/1.png'} className={'bg-img'} width={1000} height={500} alt={'Image'} />

### Step 2: Create Chat Bot
1. Click on the `Create Chat Bot` button.
<Image src={'/images/admin/ai/bots/2.png'} className={'bg-img'} width={1000} height={500} alt={'Image'} />
2. Enter the Chat Bot details.
3. Click on the `Save` button.
<Image src={'/images/admin/ai/bots/3.png'} className={'bg-img'} width={1000} height={500} alt={'Image'} />
<br/>
<Image src={'/images/admin/ai/bots/3-1.png'} className={'bg-img'} width={1000} height={500} alt={'Image'} />

### Step 3: Edit Chat Bot
1. Click on the `Edit` button to edit the Chat Bot.
<Image src={'/images/admin/ai/bots/4.png'} className={'bg-img'} width={1000} height={500} alt={'Image'} />
2. Edit the Chat Bot details.
3. Click on the `Update Changes` button.
<Image src={'/images/admin/ai/bots/5.png'} className={'bg-img'} width={1000} height={500} alt={'Image'} />
<br/>
<Image src={'/images/admin/ai/bots/5-1.png'} className={'bg-img'} width={1000} height={500} alt={'Image'} />

### Step 4: Delete Chat Bot
1. Click on the `Delete` button to delete the Chat Bot.
<Image src={'/images/admin/ai/bots/6.png'} className={'bg-img'} width={1000} height={500} alt={'Image'} />

### Step 5: Bulk Delete Chat Bots
1. Select the Chat Bots you want to delete or click on the `Select All` checkbox to select all the Chat Bots.
<Image src={'/images/admin/ai/bots/7.png'} className={'bg-img'} width={1000} height={500} alt={'Image'} />
